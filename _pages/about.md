I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in Computing and Information Sciences at Rochester Institute of Technology , and a graduate research assistant at [Global Cyber Security Institute](https://www.rit.edu/cybersecurity/). My doctoral advisor is [Dr.Mehdi Mirakhorli](https://www.se.rit.edu/~mehdi/). I got my M.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Shiraz University and worked under supervision of [Dr.Ashkan Sami](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=zIh9AvIAAAAJ&hl=en). I got my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from Azad University of Shiraz.


<h2> Research Interests</h2>
